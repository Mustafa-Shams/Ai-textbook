# Core Concepts of Physical AI

- **Embodiment**: The idea that an AI has a physical body and experiences the world through it.
- **Sensing**: How the AI perceives the world through sensors (e.g., cameras, lidar).
- **Actuation**: How the AI interacts with the world through actuators (e.g., motors, grippers).
- **Humanoid Basics**: The fundamental design and components of humanoid robots.
